Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nostr?

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) is a simple, open protocol that enables a truly censorship-resistant and global social network.

Why should I use a signer app?

A signer app keeps your private keys separate from your client applications, significantly enhancing security and reducing the risk of key theft.

Is Nostria Signer open source?

Yes, Nostria Signer is fully open-source. You can review the code, contribute, or verify security on our GitHub repository.

Which Nostr clients are supported?

Nostria Signer works with most modern Nostr clients that support NIP-46 remote signing protocol (limited to bunker://).

How does Nostria Signer store my private keys?

Nostria Signer uses your operating system's native secure storage mechanism:

Windows Credential Manager on Windows,
Keychain on macOS,
Secret Service API/kwallet on Linux,
and the Keystore system on Android.

This ensures your keys are encrypted at rest and protected by your system's security.
